docAIder

Automated Documentation Maintenance for Open-source GitHub Repositories

Say Goodbye to Manual Documentation!

Developed in partnership with University College London (UCL), and Microsoft, docAIder is designed to automate your documentation process with minimal human intervention. By integrating docAider into a development workflow, software development teams can focus on coding, while docAider takes care of generating and updating the necessary documentation. Whether it's creating new documentation, or updating it on the go, as well as based on pr comments from senior developers, docAider streamlines the entire process.
